Quick Meat and Veggie Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb ground meat (beef, turkey, or pork)
  • Splash of red wine
  • 1 cup pasata (tomato puree)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Cooked noodles

Instructions

  1. Saut√© Veggies: In a pan, fry onions and bell peppers with salt and pepper.

  2. Add Garlic: Stir in minced garlic and continue frying.

  3. Cook Meat: Add ground meat to the pan and cook until browned.

  4. Deglaze: Add a splash of red wine to deglaze the pan.

  5. Add Pasata: Stir in pasata and simmer for a few minutes.

  6. Cheese: Mix in grated Parmesan cheese.

  7. Serve: Eat the sauce over cooked noodles.

Enjoy your quick and delicious meal!

Quick Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shrimp or 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 egg
  • Bean sprouts, onions, peppers, carrots
  • 2 portions wide rice noodles
  • Lime wedge
  • Crushed peanuts, spring onions
  • 1 jar Pad Thai sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook Meat: Heat oil in a pan. Stir-fry shrimp or chicken until cooked.

  2. Add Egg: Make space in pan, crack in egg. Scramble with meat.

  3. Add Veggies and Sauce: Toss in veggies and sauce. Cook 1 min.

  4. Add Noodles: Mix in cooked noodles.

  5. Garnish: Top with peanuts, spring onions, and lime squeeze.

Enjoy!
